Tennis Analyst - (WTA)

Passionate about tennis data analysis and statistics

We are seeking a dedicated Tennis Analyst specializing in WTA to join our team.

The ideal candidate will possess a deep understanding of tennis and knowledge of tennis betting markets, with a proven track record in betting or as a tipster.

  • Conduct in-depth analysis of WTA matches and players,
  • Provide detailed reports and insights on player fitness, matchups, performance, and motivation,
  • Collaborate with other analysts to develop comprehensive data solutions,
  • Stay updated with the latest trends and developments in women's tennis.

Requirements:

  • Extensive knowledge of WTA players and tournaments,
  • Ability to produce clear, concise, and accurate analysis reports,
  • Strong analytical skills and attention to detail,
  • Knowledge of tennis betting markets with a successful betting or tipster record is a plus.

What We Offer:

  • A dynamic and collaborative work environment,
  • Competitive salary,
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.

Soccer Analyst (Poland Ekstraklasa)

Passionate about soccer data analysis and statistics

Join our team as a Freelance Soccer Analyst specializing in Poland Ekstraklasa. This role is perfect for journalists, bloggers, or anyone deeply knowledgeable about the league. We need someone who can provide detailed and accurate game previews, working remotely with flexibility. This is a fully remote position, allowing you to work from anywhere.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Create detailed previews for each Ekstraklasa game, following our guidelines,
  • Stay informed about league developments, team tactics, and specific league dynamics,
  • Rapidly find and analyze team lineups upon release.

Requirements:

  • Extensive understanding of the Poland Ekstraklasa league,
  • Good analytical and reporting abilities,
  • Availability at match times for most games in the league is essential,
  • Proficient in gathering reliable information quickly and accurately,
  • Knowledge of soccer betting markets with a successful betting or tipster record is a plus.

What We Offer:

  • A dynamic and collaborative work environment,

  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
