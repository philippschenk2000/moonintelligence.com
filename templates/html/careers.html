Developer

Short description here

We are seeking a skilled Software Developer to join our dynamic team, working remotely.

You will contribute to exciting projects involving the latest technologies in cloud services, machine learning, and scalable architectures.

  • Design, develop, and maintain applications with best practices in mind,
  • Engage in peer code reviews and technical meetings,
  • Work closely with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software,

  • Solve complex performance problems and architectural challenges.

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field,
  • Proven experience in software development,
  • Strong knowledge of modern JavaScript frameworks and tools,
  • Excellent problem-solving skills.

This position offers the flexibility to work from home with potential for international collaboration and travel.

Analyst - Soccer

Passionate about football data analysis and statistics

We are looking for a dedicated Soccer Analyst to enhance our sports analytics team.

You will analyze soccer game data to improve team performance and provide strategic insights.

  • Analyze match footage and statistical data to identify player and team trends,
  • Work with coaching staff to translate analysis into tactical strategies,
  • Prepare detailed reports on upcoming opponents and post-match reviews,
  • Develop and maintain databases of soccer statistics and performance metrics,
  • Collaborate with other departments to align strategies and support player scouting.

Qualifications:

  • Degree in Sports Science, Statistics, Data Analysis, or related field,
  • Strong understanding of soccer, including rules, teams, and player roles,
  • Proficient in the use of sports analytics software,
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills,
  • Ability to communicate complex data in an understandable way.

This position requires a passion for soccer and the ability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends.

Spontaneous Application

Think you can contribute? We’re always looking for talent!

If you don’t see a role that fits your profile, yet you believe you have something extraordinary to offer, please send us your application.

We are always on the lookout for talented individuals who can bring new perspectives and life experiences to our teams.

  • Indicate your area of expertise and how it aligns with our mission,
  • Describe unique skills and experiences that differentiate you from other candidates,
  • Highlight any previous projects or achievements relevant to our industry,
  • Express your passion for our company’s vision and your desire to contribute.

Application Tips:

  • Attach a tailored resume and a cover letter detailing your interest in our company,
  • Include a portfolio or samples of work if applicable,
  • Be creative, honest, and personable in your approach.

Spontaneous applications are reviewed on a rolling basis and matched with existing or upcoming opportunities.
